Complete Course Requirements

Self

Topic Proper Self Introduction and Parts
Activity Each student will introduce themselves using the parts of self-introduction: (Greeting, Name, Info, Traits, Hobbies, Skills, Goals, Closing).
Exam Interview a classmate by asking their name, personality, hobbies, strengths, and goals, and write down your answers.

Debate

Topic How to Debate
Activity Teacher finds a topic and divides the class into two groups. Group 1: Negative Side | Group 2: Positive Side. Students will start a debate.
Exam Search a trending topic and write arguments with evidence.

Math

Topic Why Operations are Important
Activity Solve 1000 − 419 and 1200 + 2973 with solution. Write in paper and take a photo.
Exam Solve 5555 ÷ 55 and 6676 × 2733 with solution. Write in paper and take a photo.

Roleplay

Topic How to Create Roleplay and Script
Activity Teacher divides the class into two groups and present a roleplay in group chat.
Exam Create a creative roleplay script.

Research

Topic How to Create a Research Title
Activity Students will create 3 possible research titles about school or student life.
Exam Choose the best title and explain why it is a good research title.

Reading

Topic Reading Comprehension
Activity Teacher will send a short story. Students must read it and answer 5 questions.
Exam Write a short summary of the story and explain the lesson.

Technology

Topic Basic Internet Safety
Activity Students will list 5 ways to stay safe on the internet and sent in the page.
Exam Explain why protecting passwords and personal information is important.

Creativity

Topic Creative Thinking
Activity Students will draw something that represents their dream or future.
Exam Explain the meaning of your drawing in 3–5 sentences.
